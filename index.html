Welcome to Your New Home

This charming and luminous apartment, perfectly located on Calle del Rosselló (near Lepant) in the sought-after Sagrada Família neighborhood, offers an ideal base for your studies and city adventures.

With 70 m² of well-distributed usable space, this apartment is designed for comfortable shared living. Enjoy abundant natural light throughout the day thanks to its exterior position and east orientation. Situated on the 5th floor of a well-maintained building (built in 1951) with an elevator.

  • Spacious Shared Living: Large, welcoming living and dining area (approx. 20m²).
  • Private Bedrooms: Three cozy and private bedrooms.
  • Shared Bathroom: One modern, shared bathroom with shower.
  • Functional Kitchen: Equipped with fridge/freezer, oven, hob, microwave, and washing machine.
  • Prime Location: Unbeatable access to transport (Metro L2/L5 Sagrada Família) and shops.
  • Ready to Go: Good condition, recently painted, ready for move-in.
